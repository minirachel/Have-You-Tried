feeling bad? have you tried

Have You Tried is a generator of unsolicited advice.
Yes, by loading this page and pressing the button...
it is "solicited".
But is it any more helpful?
Per usual, the details of your troubles are irrelevant.

During difficult times, "Have You Tried" can be used as a supplement to / replacement for:
  • misguided but loving family members
  • coworkers who are somehow well adjusted
  • facebook friends trapped in a MLM scheme
  • a best friend who is tired of this conversation
  • random dude on twitter
It is urgent for you to follow the suggested action
even if you have definitely already tried it.

Limit usage to twice a day
or whenever you just felt like venting.
